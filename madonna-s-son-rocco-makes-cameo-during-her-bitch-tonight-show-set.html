Don’t blink or you might miss it! Madonna rocked out with her gold grillz, leather jacket, customized “M” chains, and so much more for her ridiculously mesmerizing “Bitch I’m Madonna” performance on The Tonight Show on Thursday, April 9. But before shoving an audience member’s face near her crotch and sprawling out on Jimmy Fallon’s desk (yes, all of this happened!), the superstar’s 13-year-old son Rocco made a cameo.

As Queen Madge, 56, makes her sexy way down the hallway to the show’s stage, she pops into several dressing rooms to shake it with her backup dancers and collaborator Diplo. After one choreographed move, Rocco jumps in front of the camera for a quick solo. Giving lip syncing a try, he mouths the lyric: “Bitch, I’m Madonna!”
