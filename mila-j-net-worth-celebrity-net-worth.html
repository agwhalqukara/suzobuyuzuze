What is Mila J's Net Worth?

Mila J is an American singer, songwriter, rapper, and dancer who has a net worth of $4 million. Mila J was born in Los Angeles, California in November 1983. Her younger sister is R&B singer Jhene Aiko. Mila J was featured in the video "Diamond and Pearls" by Prince and has appeared with other artists including Trey Songz, RaRa, Omarion, and IMx.
