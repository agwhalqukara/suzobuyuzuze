Bali Ram Bhagat College, affiliated with Lalit Narayan Mithila University, is a beacon of education in Darbhanga, offering programs in Arts, Science, and Commerce up to the Honors level. Situated on a serene 6-acre campus at the South-West end of Samastipur, this co-ed college harmoniously blends the natural environment with urban conveniences. With modern amenities such as a well-equipped Science Block, an extensive library, and spacious common rooms, it is committed to providing quality education. Bali Ram Bhagat College’s mission is to empower and uplift the underprivileged while fostering a sense of security and belonging in a strategic location that draws students from both urban and rural backgrounds.

Check out the instructions for filling up an online admission form for an intermediate program. Here’s a step-by-step process for login into the website:

Prerequisites:

  • You must have a mobile number and email ID.
  • These will be used for registration and communication throughout the admission process.

Registration:

  • Click on the “Intermediate Admission Register” link as a first-time student.
  • Fill out all the required fields on the registration page. Fields marked with an asterisk (*) are mandatory.
  • An OTP (One-Time Password) will be sent to your mobile number for confirmation.
  • Enter the OTP to confirm your registration.

Login:

  • After successful registration, log in by clicking on the “Intermediate Admission Login” link.

Application Form:

  • The online application form is divided into five tabs or sections/pages.
  • Enter the required details accurately in each section.
  • Before submitting, there should be an option to preview the information.
  • Review the information on the Preview page and make any necessary corrections using the modification option.

Document Upload:

  • Prepare the necessary documents in scanned copy format (.JPG) with specific requirements:
    • Scanned Photograph: Dimensions 200 pixels width x 230 pixels height, not more than 50 KB.
    • Scanned Signature: Dimensions 140 pixels width x 60 pixels height, not more than 30 KB.
    • Scanned BSEB Selection Letter: Size not more than 150 KB.
    • Scanned Class 10th Marksheet: Size not more than 150 KB.
    • Scanned Last School/College CLC/TC/SLC: Size not more than 150 KB.
    • Scanned Caste/EWS Certificate (if applicable): Size not more than 150 KB.
    • Scanned Income Certificate (by the competent authority, if applicable): Size not more than 150 KB.
    • Scanned Aadhaar: Size not more than 150 KB.

Payment:

  • The admission fees vary by stream:
    • Science: Rs. 1048.00
    • Arts: Rs. 1035.00
    • Commerce: Rs. 1035.00
  • Make the payment online using one of the provided options (Credit Card, Debit Card, Internet Banking, or UPI).

Confirmation:

  • After successful payment, you should receive confirmation of your application submission.

Make sure to double-check all the information you provide, as inaccuracies may cause issues with your application. Follow these steps carefully, and you should be able to complete your online admission form for the intermediate program successfully.
