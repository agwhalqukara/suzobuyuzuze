James McVey To Release T-Shirts For Centrepoint Charity

August 07, 2024
blog

James McVey is no stranger to trying to make a difference in the world. From speaking on LBC about issues many face to anti-bullying chats on Sky News. Now he’s taken on the challenge of raising money for Centrepoint, a charity specialising in helping homeless young people.

Recently James has been teasing followers on social media with images of two super cute and quirky t-shirt designs. In a short video posted to his Instagram, James announced that these two shirts will be released on Friday for a limited time with all profit going to Centrepoint.

https://www.instagram.com/p/BImwl3DAHdK/?taken-by=iamjamesmcvey

Centrepoint supports over 7800 young people each year across London and the North East who would otherwise find themselves out on the street. Alongside providing health support and education to allow them to get their lives back on track and set them up for the future. James has always made it clear charity is close to our heart, and they couldn’t be more deserving of the money set to be raised.

Don’t forget to order your shirt on Friday 5th August via the link James will be posting over the coming days. The shirts are priced around £18 each, with all profits going to Centrepoint.

To find out more about Centrepoint and the brilliant work they do, head to their website at Centrepoint.org.uk and find out what else you can do to help out.
