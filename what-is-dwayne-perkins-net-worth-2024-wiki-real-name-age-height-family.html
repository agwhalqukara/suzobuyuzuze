FAQs About Dwayne Perkins

Who is Dwayne Perkins?

Dwayne Perkins is an American stand-up comedian known for his unique perspective on life and his ability to convey it humorously.

A Brooklyn native, Perkins has made significant strides in the comedy scene with appearances on Conan, Comedy Central, and The Jay Leno Show.

What are some of his notable appearances?

He has appeared on Late Night with Conan O’Brien, The Late Late Show with Craig Ferguson, Premium Blend, and has his own Comedy Central Presents half-hour special. He has performed in various locations, including New York, Boston, and Los Angeles.

Has he received any accolades for his comedy?

Yes, he was selected by Rolling Stone magazine as one of their Five Comics to Watch in August 2012. His Comedy Central half-hour special was also voted one of the top 25 Comedy Central specials of all time.

Does he have any comedy specials available online?

He has a one-hour comedy special titled Dwayne Perkins: Take Note that debuted on Netflix to rave reviews. It quickly became a fan favorite and was in the top 50 Netflix comedy specials of all time only 2 months after its release.

What is his approach to comedy?

He focuses on making his comedy accessible to a wide range of audiences without alienating anyone. He believes in coming back stronger after bombing on stage and values the immediate feedback from live performances to improve his material.

Has he written any books?

Yes, he is the author of Hot Chocolate For the Mind, a collection of humorous essays, and Zombie Run, a post-apocalyptic fiction novel.

What is his background?
